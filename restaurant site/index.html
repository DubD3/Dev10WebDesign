In 2019, Will started living a healthy lifestyle in Internet City, MN. Experimenting different healthy recipes and cooking techniques. In 2020 Will decided to turn his passion in to a business and opened Healthy Delights.

Through hard work, persistence & delightful recipes and quality service, his healthy business grew into one of the most popular in the city, and soon had outgrown its previous location and prompted the move and expansion to its new downtown location.

Healthy Delight's Restaurant in Internet City, MN is locally owned. We have a staff of over 30 of the friendliest & health conscious people in Internet City community. We look forward to serving you our from-scratch healthy food with high quality service!
